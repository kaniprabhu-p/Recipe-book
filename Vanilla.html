REDVELVET CAKE

Description:-

Cooking Time

  1. Preparation Time :25
  2. Cook Time : 30 Min
  3. Total Time : 55 Min

Ingredients:-

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup powder sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp vanila essence
  • 2 cup whipped cream
  • 1 cup sugar syrup
  • 1 drop of Pink,green and black colour

Direction:-

    Beat butter and sugar till creamy and fluffy Add one by one egg and beat Add vanila essence Sieve flour and baking powder Mix dry ingredients in wet and fold them Grease a mould and line If battar is thick add some milk Mix finely and pour battar in mould Bake 25 to 30 min.at 180 degree When bake let it cool Take sugar syrup and brush on cake Cover with whip cream for crumb coating Keep in freeze for 1 hour Now take fondant and add green pink and black colour in small portions Roll down white one and cover cake Set it on cake carefully Remove extra parts Now make quilling with coloured fondant Stick on cake with help of water Add green Colour in whip cream And pipe outside the cake Our cake is ready to serve

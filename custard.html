CUSTARD CAKE

Description:-

Cooking Time

  1. Preparation Time :10
  2. Min Cook Time : 40 Min
  3. Total Time : 50 Min

Ingredients:-

  • Wheat flour -1.25cups
  • Vanilla custard powder 1/4th cup
  • Baking powder -1tsp
  • Baking soda-1/2tsp
  • Condensed milk -3/4 th cup or 1cup
  • Butter softened - 1/4 cup
  • Pinch of salt 1/4 teaspoon se bhi kam
  • Almond essence -3 - 4 drops
  • Vanilla essence -1.5tsp
  • Curd-1/2 cup
  • Milk-1/2 cup
  • Chopped dry fruit 5-6 tablespoons

Direction:-

Preheat oven @ 150degree.Greece the cake baking tin and dust with flour. Sift flour cornflour baking powder baking soda salt , 3 to 4 times n keep it aside.In a bowl add softened butter condensed milk, beat well with hand mixer.Stir in milk curd vanilla essence almond essence and mix well.To this, fold in flour in 2 batches if the batter is thick add little milk n mix. Pour the batter into the prepared tin, put chopped dry fruit on top and bake it in preheated oven for 40 minutes.
